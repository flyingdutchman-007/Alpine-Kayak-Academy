Course description Book now
In our training course we want to get your kayaking to a new level, from beginner to advanced. We want to give you the tools needed to run Alpin whitewater effortless and with style. We focus on training you the newest techniques and reading the river like no other!
With over 10 years of experience in coaching whitewater we want to share all our knowledge about running rivers with you!
Course information
Our location
Our training course takes place in the heart of the Julian Alps, where the world class Soča river is located. The emerald Soča river is one of the most stunning places in the Alps and offers great training possibilities for our courses! With over 10 years of guiding on this river we know all the little spots where we can boost your kayaking to the next level.
Skill level required
We take beginners to advanced kayakers. If you have never paddled white water before, you want to catch those eddies with more style or need to work on your boof. We do it all and for everyone! We strongly believe in personalized training, everyone is different, that is why we will get in touch with you to design the best training program to ensure the best progress possible.
Our techniques
We want to boost your kayaking with training different skills. We strongly believe that teaching modern technique is the best way to go and makes kayaking easier. Modern style includes different type of paddle strokes like power-, forward- and steering strokes. We want to teach you how to use the upper- and lower body in combination with these strokes to make your kayak do what you want to. We also pay attention to read waterlines to make sure you know how to use them in your advantage!
Our equipment
We care a great deal about having the best and newest equipment. This makes sure our kayak course can be taught with the newest techniques and in the safest way possible. This includes boats, paddles, pfd, dry jacket, ect. If you bring your own equipment we will make sure that it fits our safety standard.
