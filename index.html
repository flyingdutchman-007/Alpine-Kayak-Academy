Hello, we are Alpin Kayak Academy

We are a small team of licensed trainers that give personalized training for individuals and groups. Our goal is to get your kayaking skills to the next level with providing training in the newest techniques and kayaking gear. Our base is located in the Soca Valley and the courses take place on the Soca river, which we know like the back of our hand. We also offer kayak trips to various places in Europe, where you can explore new rivers in a safe environment. We are super flexibel, if you have any questions please contact us!
