Our great whitewater trainings course

Course description Book a course
In our training course we want to get your kayaking to a new level, from beginner to advanced. We want to give you the tools needed to run Alpin white-water effortless and with style. We focus on training you the newest techniques and reading the river like no other!
With over 10 years of experience in coaching whitewater we want to share all our knowledge about running Alpin rivers with you!
Course information
Our location
Our training course takes place in the heart of the Julian Alps, where the world class Soca river is located. The emerald Soca river is one of the most stunning places in the Alps and offers some great training possibilities! With over 10 years of guiding on this emerald river we know all the little spots where we can boost your kayaking to the next level.
Skill level required
We take beginners to advanced kayakers. If you have never paddled white water before, you want to catch those eddies with more style or need to work on your boof. We do it all and for everyone! We strongly believe in personalized training, everyone is different, that is why we will get in touch with you to design the best training program to ensure the best progress possible.
Our goals
We want to boost your kayaking with training different skills. We strongly believe that teaching modern technique is the best way to go and makes kayaking easier. Modern style includes different type of paddle strokes like power-, forward- and steering strokes. With these paddles strokes body movement is a key element. We want to teach you how to use the upper- and lower body in combination with these strokes to make your kayak do what you want to. We also pay attention to reading white water to use different features on the water in your advantage!
Equipment
If you don’t have any equipment, don’t worry! In our training course package, the equipment is included. Do you have your own equipment? Then you are free to bring it to our trainings course. We will make sure to proof the status of your safety equipment, so that we are sure that we are all safe and sound on the river. We will give you some discount on our trainings course package!
